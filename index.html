We are a socially responsible enterprise, committed to transforming the security industry while empowering non-skilled workers financially and through skill development. In an effort to achieve our goals, we have initiated a ground-breaking ownership structure where 40% of the company is owned by an investment Sacco comprised of our staff members. This unique setup allows our employees to become owners of the business, promotes savings, and encourages them to acquire essential skills in the field. In addition, at the close of each financial year, Wingrand shares dividends with the Sacco, enabling employees to partake in the company's success. Furthermore, the investment Sacco will oversee a Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) program, offering subsidized skills development to its members.

Our mission is to create a secure environment for our clients and to provide economic opportunities and skills to our employees.

To fully realize the potential of innovative and impactful business model, we are seeking strategic partners who share our vision for social entrepreneurship, economic empowerment, and sustainable security services. Your engagement of our guards will not only support the growth and expansion of Wingrand Security Limited but also make a meaningful contribution to the empowerment of non-skilled workers in the security industry.
